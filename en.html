Konstantinov Konstantin Konstantinovich, born in 1968.
In 2001 I graduated from the St. Petersburg Repin Academy of Arts. One of the best art universities in Russia.
Since 2002, member of the Union of Artists of Russia.
I teach at the Stieglitz Art Academy in the rank of professor.
I engage in creative activities, participate in government projects, laureate of international and national exhibitions.

Some of the significant projects:
  • Participation in the painting of the Cathedral of Christ the Savior in Moscow (the largest temple in Russia).
  • Reconstruction of the Kronstadt Naval Cathedral. Supervised a collective of 250 artists and painted the upper and lower temples.
Awards:
  • Medal of the Imperial Order of St. Anne, III degree.
  • Medal on the reconstruction of the Kronstadt Naval Cathedral.
  • Patriarchal letter from Kirill.
  • Silver medal of the Russian Academy of Arts (2014).
  • and other awards..
About myself:
I am painting because I like it, it is my passion. I like to paint portraits because each person is individual and keeps many secrets. When I paint antiquity, I try to preserve history for posterity. I try to go to the plein air more often because in the pictures created from nature there is more energy and life.
